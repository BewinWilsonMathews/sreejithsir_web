Profile

Sreejith S Nair ( M.Sc, M.phil (Organic Chemistry), (M.phil )(Applied Psychology)) is the founder & chairman of KBS Academy, a pioneer NEET/JEE Entrance Coaching centre in Palakkad District,Kerala.

KBS Academy holds Rank No.1 position in the district especially in NEET Ranks. More than 1000 students are appearing for exams in every year from the academy. KBS Academy has its branches at Palakkad Town, Palakkad Pirayiri , Ottapalam & Mannarkkad. More than 100 students are learning from outside kerala and around 50 students are from outside India in this academy. KBS Academy is providing free education to poor students in association with Police department of Kerala under the project “HOPE”. Also KBS Academy is the only institution in Palakkad with its own learning app .

Sreejith S Nair , being an educationist and an excellent motivational trainer conducted more than 1000 motivational training sessions for students of Kerala & Tamilnadu, who are going to face board exams and entrance exams. He also conducted several training programmes for teachers also in how to become an inspiring teacher.He won several awards for his contribution in uplifting the educational standard of Palakkad district from various government agencies & NGO s. He worked as assistant professor in engineering colleges for nearly 8 years in the initial stage of his career and this experience also gets counted to his skills in mentoring and motivating students.

He is the past president of Junior Chamber International, Palghat & won the most outstanding president for the year 2020 among three districts in Kerala. He was the most outstanding JCI member in the year 2019. He is the secretary of Lions club of Palakkad Green City in the year 2020-21. He is the Vice President of Rotary Club of Palakkad Fort in the year 2020-21. He is also a member of Palakkad Trainers Forum .

In 2018, he delivered motivational training sessions for more than 50 schools under the project “Reach Out 10,000”. In the year 2019, he delivered Successful Exam Preparation sessions for nearly 100 schools under the project “Ignite to Impact”.In 2019 itself he conducted “Rotary Youth Leadership Academy “ sessions in more than 10 schools under the Rotary project “RYLA”. In the year 2020-21 , he is delivering motivational session module “ Plan –B for Students to face exams in this Pandemic Situation”.

He is the pilot faculty of signature “Organic Chemistry Work Shop “ – for Plus Two students in which all the tips & tricks to crack organic chemistry reactions will be shared with the students for the past 10 years.

He is happily married to Mrs . Navajyothi Sreejith ( M.Sc, B.Ed ) and blessed with two daughters-Nanma S Nair & Punya S Nair .
